About Flora Glam

The Artist Behind the Brush

Hi, I'm Flora

I started my journey in the beauty industry with a simple mission: to help everyone see the most beautiful version of themselves. With years of experience specializing in high-definition makeup, I bring a touch of luxury and precision to every client.

My philosophy is that makeup should never be a mask. Instead, it’s a tool to highlight your unique features. From bridal elegance to editorial bold looks, I am dedicated to providing a personalized experience that leaves you feeling confident and radiant.
